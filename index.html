ABOUT US

The Best Holidays Start Here!

Nestled in the heart of Ooty, MK Guest House offers a serene and comfortable retreat for travelers seeking a memorable stay. Our guest house blends modern amenities with a touch of traditional charm, ensuring a pleasant and relaxing experience for all our guests.

INFRASTRUCTURE:

Our guest house features a range of thoughtfully designed rooms and suites, each equipped with contemporary furnishings and all the necessary conveniences. The architecture of the building combines elegance and functionality, creating a welcoming atmosphere.

Guests can enjoy:

  • Spacious Rooms: Well-appointed rooms with comfortable beds, ample storage, and en-suite bathrooms.
  • Common Areas: Relax in our cozy lounge, read a book in our quiet library, or enjoy a meal in our dining area.
  • Outdoor Spaces: Beautifully landscaped gardens, a terrace with stunning views, and outdoor seating areas for relaxation.
  • Facilities:We strive to provide our guests with a wide range of facilities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay
  • Free Wi-Fi: Stay connected with complimentary high-speed internet access throughout the property.
  • Breakfast Included: Start your day with a delicious and hearty breakfast served in our dining area.
  • 24/7 Front Desk: Our friendly and professional staff are available around the clock to assist you with any needs or inquiries.
  • Room Service: Enjoy the convenience of in-room dining with our extensive room service menu.
  • Laundry Service: Take advantage of our laundry facilities to keep your clothes fresh and clean during your stay.
  • Secure Parking: Ample parking space is available for guests with vehicles, ensuring your peace of mind.
